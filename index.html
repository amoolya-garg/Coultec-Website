MOTOR

The inverter system is integral to the performance of our motors, with options for both single and split inverter packages, depending on the number and type of motors used. Each motor is paired with a dedicated inverter, offering a more affordable solution without compromising performance. The motor controlling unit (MCU) includes a processor that manages motor functions, ensuring precise control and efficiency. For additional customization, we provide software that can be tuned to optimize performance based on the customer's available hardware. Our cooling solutions are engineered by the manufacturer, making heat management straightforward and effective for seamless integration into any application.
